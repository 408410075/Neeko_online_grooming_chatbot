【 👉 What A Predator Might Do 】
There are six stages of online grooming:
1️⃣ Friendship Forming Stage
The predator approaches children, and at the same time collects as much personal information as possible on the Internet to be sure that the target is a child.

2️⃣ Relationship Forming Stage
The predator continues to gather information about the children to make a relationship with them.

3️⃣ Risk Assessment Stage
The predator assesses the danger of his communication with the child, such as whether his conversation with the child will be seen by others or the family's concern for the children's online behavior, etc., to be sure that his behavior will not be exposed.

4️⃣ Exclusivity Stage
The predator introduces the concept of love and care to the child, then makes a more personal and special relationship.

5️⃣ Sexual Stage
At this time, the predator will gradually introduce sexual topics and may send his nude photos or ask the child to send nude photos, videos, etc.

6️⃣ Conclusion Stage
The predator attempts to arrange meetings with the child in person for real-life sexual exploitation.

【 👉 網路誘拐者可能會做什麼事？ 】
網路誘拐共有六個階段：

1️⃣ 友誼形成階段
誘拐者向兒少接近，同時盡可能地蒐集兒少在網路上的個人訊息，以確認對象為兒童。

2️⃣ 關係形成階段：誘拐者持續蒐集兒少的訊息，並以此與他們建立關係。

3️⃣ 風險評估階段：誘拐者評估自己與兒少的交流的危險性，例如他與兒少的對話是否會被他人查看、家人對於兒少網路交友的關懷程度等，以確認自己的行為不會曝光。

4️⃣ 排他性階段：誘拐者向兒少提出愛與關懷的概念，與兒少建立更私人與特殊的關係。

5️⃣ 性階段：此時誘拐者會逐漸引入與「性」相關的話題，可能會傳送自己的或是要求兒少傳送裸露照片、影像等。

6️⃣結束階段：誘拐者開始安排與兒少現實中的會面，以進行現實中的性剝削。
